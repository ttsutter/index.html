Malignant is a 2021 American horror film directed by James Wan from a screenplay by Akela Cooper, based on an original story by Wan, Ingrid Bisu, and Cooper. The film stars Annabelle Wallis as a woman who begins to have visions of people being murdered, only to realize the events are happening in real life. We rate is movie a 7/10 and would definetly recommend to watch!

Malignant is avaliable to stream on Amazon Prime currently but should be re-added to HBO MAX at the start of 2022!
